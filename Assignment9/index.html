Chipotle Burrito Duplication Glitch

Step 1

Head to your local chipotle!

Step 2

Order a Burrtio Bowl rather than a burrito!

  • A bowl costs the same amount as a burrito and can fit more toppings inside!
  • Order all the toppings you'd like. (You can get certain extra toppings for free)
  • Be sure to order 2 tortillas with it for $1!
  • If you'd like queso be sure to order it on the side rather than on the bowl!

Step 3

Return home!

Step 4

Use half of the bowl to make a burrito using your tortillas

  • Use the other half to make a second burrito and you are left with 2 burritos for a little over the price of one!
